Welcome To Secco Ceramica.

Secco Ceramica defines elegance and beauty in the tile industry with its superb quality tiles, which generate values. Secco Ceramica is the leading manufacturer and supplier of digital wall tiles, vitrified (Porcelain) tiles, and floor tiles.

01. Laying Over Gravel
Create a layer of at least 4 Inches of gravel with a diameter of 1/4" on the existing substrate. Level of the surface with straightedge and check the flatness.
Place the tile on the gravel and check the gap between them is same over the entire laying surface. Tap down gently with a rubber mallet to settle them in to place and eliminate an unevenness.
Add gravel in joints to level the flooring area. Decorative gravel that is larger in diameter can be used instead of what is usedÂ forÂ substrate
02. Laying Over Grass
Place tile on the grass and check that the gap between them is same over entire laying area.
Mark out the area of turf the removed and dig out a depth of at least 4 inches. Create a layer of at least 3 inches of gravel (dia. 3/4"). Compact and level the gravel
Lay sand on a layer at least 1 inch thick, maintain the space between sand layer and surface of the ground to 20mm (tile thickness). Compact and level the sand.
Put the tile in place and tap down gently with a rubber mallet to settle them in to place. Correct the unevennessÂ ifÂ any.
03. Laying Over Sand
Prepare a layer of sand at least 1 inch thick on existing substrate (concrete or Gravel). Level the surface with a straightedge and check the flatness.
Place the tile on sand and use spacer to ensure an even gap between tiles in entire laying area (minimum advisableÂ gapÂ isÂ 1/8")
04. Laying As Raised Floor
Clean the existing concrete surface, ensure it is dry and cured and check the level.
Prepare the selected pedestals, cut those which will be placed at edge of the tiles to maintain the edge line flat.
Place the pedestals on concrete floor in grid as per laying diagram firstly install the pedestals in all edges
If any wall is connected to the flooring, Fix the tile edge protective clips on the pedestals in that area.
